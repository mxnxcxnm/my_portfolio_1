MonicaNMamboundou PORTFOLIO PROJECTS ⟳
POLITICAL RISK ANALYSIS (WORLD BANK) — METHODOLOGY & RESULTS

METHODOLOGY

  • Frameworks: Guided Exercise exploring probability concepts and their applications, sampling, visualisation, ratio estimation and hypothesis testing.
  • Sources: Datasets from the World Bank Open Data Portal. Specifically, "Political Stability and Absence of Violence" indicator from 1996 to 2023.

RESULTS & INSIGHTS

  • Europe & Central Asia and Latin America & Caribbean are mutually exclusive with low income. North America is mutually exclusive with any income group, excluding high-income. South Asia is mutually exclusive with High income.
  • The ratio estimation informs us that over a third of countries in the world can be considered stable in 2023 i.e., have a stability score superior to 0.5.
  • The conclusion from the hypothesis test is that the mean stability scores in Europe and Central Asia and the mean in Sub-saharan Africa are significantly different at 0.22 and-0.67 respectively.
